Sim Sickness Symptoms- Oculomotor

  • Headaches (17%)
  • Fatigue (27%-43%)
  • Eye strain (29%-37%)
  • Can't focus

Sim Sickness Symptoms - Nausea

  • Sweating (30%)
  • Salivation
  • Can't concentrate
  • Burping/stomach awareness

Sim Sickness Symptoms - Disorientation

  • Blurry vision
  • Dizziness (with eyes open or closed)
  • Vertigo (24%)

Sim Sickness Causes - Technical

  • Low frame rate (Academics/military struggle with this!!)
  • High latency
  • Image Persistence
  • No Head Tracking

Sim Sickness Causes - Environment

  • History of MS
  • Age
  • Gender
  • Alcohol use
  • Flu, cold, hangover

Sim Sickness In-Game

  • Acceleration (turning)
  • Collisions
  • Head movement
  • Fixing an image on the screen (Loading screens)
  • "Moving large components of the environment"

Sim Sickness In-Game

  • Putting your head through a wall
  • Moving out of head tracking range
  • Moving at a rapid speed
  • Unrealistic avatar

about *every* screen
